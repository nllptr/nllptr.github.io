My YouTube Debut

PUBLISHED ON NOV 12, 2016 — GO

So I’ve made my YouTube debut. Well, actually I made a series of videos a few years ago, but that was on a totally different topic. They’re still up there on my channel if you happen to be interested in creating chiptunes.

But what are these new videos about? First of all, I created a series called “Watch me learn Go”, which is about just that. Me learning Go. I intend for this series to be mostly recorded programming sessions, since that makes it easy to produce. Just record and upload, that’s the idea. The first set of videos are me creating a program that creates animated GIFs with Conway’s “Game of Life”.

At some point I will probably go back and tag these as “season 1”, and then make each project its own season. If you want to check out the playlist, you can do so here.

I have also recorded the first part of an ongoing series called “Golang Gotchas”, in which I will dive in to gotchas and stumbling blocks that I’ve come across as I learn more about Go programming. You can watch my Golang Gotchas here.

See ya!
